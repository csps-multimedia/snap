Introduction to module

Managers at all levels, along with accredited human resources (HR) advisors, have a role to play with regard to organization and classification (OC).

This module presents the basic principles of OC to assist you in carrying out your day-to-day duties in this field and in fulfilling your responsibilities. The next modules set out the principles associated with organizational structure, job descriptions, and classification decisions.

This training is mandatory for those who will have responsibility or authority for organization and classification, as outlined in the Organization and Classification Learning Curriculum..
