Welcome to Module 2 – Key Concepts and Principles

The purpose of this module is to outline the key concepts and principles of security. It provides an introduction to the Policy on Government Security, and its associated key principles. Your department may have more detailed departmental policies and standards that are specific to your mandate and mission.

Objectives

Upon completion of this session, you will be able to:

  • Identify relevant security policies, standards and guidelines
  • Recognize the need for security in an organization
  • List key security roles and responsibilities
