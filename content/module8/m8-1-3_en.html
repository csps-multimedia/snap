Compromise

When a security breach takes place, a compromise may occur.

A compromise is the unauthorized access to, (or) disclosure, modification, use, interruption, removal, or destruction of sensitive information or assets, causing a loss of confidentiality, integrity, availability of value. A compromise could result in damage or injury to individuals, businesses, government operations, and services, or the national interest.

Key Message

When a compromise occurs, it is extremely important to quickly report the incident to your manager or departmental security office, so that your department can identify and assess the degree of injury, notify those who are impacted and take corrective actions.
