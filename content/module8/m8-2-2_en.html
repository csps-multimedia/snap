Reporting Requirements

Depending on the type and severity of the incident, your departmental security office may be required to submit a report to central agencies or the Canadian Security Intelligence Service (CSIS) or the Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP). When the investigation has identified a material privacy breach, institutions must provide formal written notification to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner and TBS.
