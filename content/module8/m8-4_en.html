Investigations

An investigation will be conducted when significant issues arise regarding policy compliance, allegations of misconduct, suspected criminal activity, security incidents, or workplace violence. An investigation is a comprehensive process to examine all the circumstances surrounding an incident. If evidence of wrongdoing is found, investigation may recommend disciplinary action.

There are different types of investigations:

Administrative Investigations

Administrative investigations are a formal process conducted within the department. They are carried out with the purpose of establishing, documenting and analyzing all the relevant facts in order to identify the cause, the damage and potential corrective actions, and allow management to make an informed decision regarding the course of action.

Criminal Investigations

In some instances, a security incident may result in a criminal investigation by the appropriate law enforcement authority. In such cases, the personnel involved are required to cooperate with police accordingly.
