Reporting Security Events

It is extremely important that you immediately report all security incidents through the appropriate channels so that action can be taken to limit or prevent harm.

Every department should have its own reporting procedures and contacts. It is your responsibility to know these procedures and to know whom to call if an incident occurs. If you are not sure, ask your manager or departmental security office.

As some departments may have different protocols in place, you should also be aware how to report workplace safety and hazardous occurrences within your specific department.

You should call 911 immediately if your personal safety is at risk due to an act of violence, fire or health emergency.

Key Message

Do not wait until an incident occurs. Make sure you know what to do.
