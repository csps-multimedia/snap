Working Remotely (Teleworking)

Some users are authorized to work at home or while travelling. They work remotely from various locations. “Teleworking” is an authorized work arrangement for an employee to perform the duties of their position off-site while being able to access the organization's network over the Internet. Since organizations no longer have physical control of the user's work environment, there are additional security risks.

Departments that allow personnel to access departmental information and networks and systems remotely must establish procedures for such use. IT security will advise you of your departmental policy.

For example, you should use a government-owned computer with appropriate security software such as a Firewall, antivirus software and Secure Remote Access software.

There is a government standard secure remote access solution that enables you to securely log-in to your departmental network via the Internet.

Additional Safeguards while Working Remotely (Teleworking)

Some additional safeguards must be observed when working from home or other remote locations, as follows:

  • Be aware of your surroundings when discussing sensitive information;
  • Be conscious of “shoulder surfing” when working in public spaces;
  • Never allow any non-staff or unauthorized personnel to use any of your government devices;
  • Do not send work from your workplace to your home via public e-mail sites such as Hotmail or Yahoo. These sites are not secure and should never be used for business purposes.
  • Use caution when using free-public access WiFi; and
  • NEVER communicate unencrypted sensitive information over WiFi networks

When in doubt, ask your departmental IT security coordinator for more information.

Teleworking

A flexible work arrangement whereby employees have approval to carry out some or all of their work duties from a telework place.

Telework place

The alternative location where the employee is permitted to carry out the work otherwise performed at or from their designated workplace.
