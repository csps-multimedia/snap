Policy on Acceptable Networks and Device Use

Your department should have acceptable use policies that based on its mandate and that are in line with the Policy on Acceptable Networks and Device Use.

Your department is required to inform all authorized individuals on:

  • Expectations for acceptable use of Government of Canada electronic networks and devices
  • Electronic network monitoring practices being applied
  • Consequences for unacceptable use of such networks and devices

You will be expected to formally acknowledge that you have read and understand these requirements.
