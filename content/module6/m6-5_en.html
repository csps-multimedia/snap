After a long weekend, Michael returns to the office. He has planned to continue working on an important report his manager asked him to draft.

When Michael turns on his computer he notices that his system is unusually slow, his e-mail software does not start, and his word processor crashes frequently.

What should Michael do in this situation?

Select the most appropriate answer.

Your answer is correct!

Michael should contact his department’s help desk.
