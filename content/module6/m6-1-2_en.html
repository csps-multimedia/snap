Complying with Security Requirements

Although the government is evaluating increased use of the Internet and web 2.0 tools and services under certain circumstances, authorized users remain responsible for complying with security requirements. Sensitive information must be protected. You should never post Protected or Classified information on unprotected sites such as GCpedia, social media, or other third-party web tools such as Google docs. You must also ensure that you are authorized to share government information before you post any information on these sites.
