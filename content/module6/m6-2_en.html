While browsing the website of a leading university, Miranda finds a great online resource that could help the members of her team improve their performance. She is so excited that she decides to download it directly to her computer to share later with everyone else.

Miranda starts the download and leaves for lunch. She does not realize that this resource is so big that downloading it slows down her department’s network and affects the ability of others to perform their work effectively.

Your answer is correct!

This is unacceptable use of electronic networks and devices.

Although Miranda is downloading a resource to help her team, her action could impact negatively on the performance of her department’s electronic networks. Not only could she unknowingly imped departmental operations and the delivery of services, she could create a potential security risk to the departmental network.

Miranda should have consulted with her Information Technology group.
