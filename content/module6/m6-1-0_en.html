Acceptable Use of Electronic Networks and Devices

Access to government electronic networks and devices is granted to authorized individuals for the conduct of official business, including activities that are necessary for our job functions.

Open access to the Internet, including external Web 2.0 tools and services, will enhance productivity, communication and collaboration, and encourage the sharing of knowledge and expertise to support innovation.

Access is also permitted for career development and other professional activities, and for limited personal use.

All use of Government of Canada electronic networks and devices must be in compliance with the Values and Ethics Code for the Public Sector and all other related TBS policies and departmental codes of conduct and policies. Use of Government of Canada electronic networks and devices must not give rise to a real, potential or apparent conflict of interest or in any way undermine the integrity of your department.

You must therefore understand what constitutes unlawful or unacceptable activity.
