Personal Effects

As a preventive measure, employees are encouraged to take steps to protect their personal belongings, as well as departmental assets. It is your responsibility to protect your property; your organization cannot be held liable for loss or theft, or for vandalism to personal property. Security officials will help investigate, so it is important to report losses, thefts or vandalism immediately to your manager or departmental security office.

To protect your property:

  • lock up valuables in a drawer or cabinet
  • never leave unattended handbags, wallets, cheques or cash exposed
  • carry your keys with you or have them secured in your section
  • mark your name on tempting and/or valuable personal articles
