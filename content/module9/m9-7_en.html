Threats or Intimidation

A threat or act of intimidation may require urgent attention, especially if it happens more than once. Never dismiss an inappropriate action or uncomfortable situation as a non-event. Quickly assess the situation, but remain calm. Safely distance yourself if you feel an immediate threat to yourself or someone else.

Report the incident immediately as it could be more serious than you think. Timely reporting is essential because the incident could affect other employees in the office or at other work locations. If the action does not represent an immediate threat, document the event.

If under a threat of physical violence:

  • contact police through 911 (if the incident is an emergency)
  • notify your manager
  • notify security services
