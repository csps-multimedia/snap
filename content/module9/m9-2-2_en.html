Roles in Prevention of Workplace Violence

Role of Employee:

  • to promptly report any incident where there is a likelihood of workplace violence to their supervisor/manager
  • to cooperate in the investigation and resolution of matters involving workplace violence
  • to attend workplace violence prevention information/awareness sessions and any other violence prevention activities

Role of Employer

  • to ensure the violence prevention policy is implemented, available and training has been provided to all employees
  • to respond promptly and take all appropriate actions following any report of workplace violence.

If you are a victim, or if you suspect workplace violence, contact your manager or departmental security office.
