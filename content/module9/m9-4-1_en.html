Employees: Targets of Outside Influences

Government employees are often too quick to dismiss the notion that they could be targets. On the contrary, your perceived access to classified or privileged government information makes you an attractive target. As such, you are seen as a source of information by foreign intelligence services or operatives attempting to harvest that information or by domestic organized crime cells looking to gain access to government databases.

Many countries have sophisticated eavesdropping/intercept technologies capable of collecting the information we aim to protect, especially overseas.

Vigilance is necessary!
