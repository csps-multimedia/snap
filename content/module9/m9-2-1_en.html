Workplace Violence

Examples of Workplace Violence include:

  • aggressive behaviour using a weapon
  • sexual violence
  • hitting, shoving, pushing or kicking
  • destroying property, throwing objects
  • expression of an intent to inflict harm
  • verbal abuse, swearing, insults or condescending language

Workplace violence is a serious matter; it can affect all employees, not just those directly impacted by an incident. Employers have a legal obligation to provide a violence-free workplace.
