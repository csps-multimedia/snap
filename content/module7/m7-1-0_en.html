What is Security in Contracts?

It is the measures put in place to ensure that sensitive information, assets and facilities entrusted to or shared with organizations outside the federal government under contractual or non-contractual arrangements are protected at all phases of the contracting process or arrangement.

It is a means of ensuring that individuals and companies doing business with the Government of Canada meet the criteria established to assure the government that they do not pose an unacceptable risk to personnel, information, assets, and operations in the conduct of the proposed contract work.

Likewise, it is also a means of ensuring that sensitive information and assets shared with external partners are properly protected (e.g. sharing information with research institutions or universities as part of a study).
