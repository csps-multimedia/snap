Cautionary Notes

There are dedicated sections within PSPC, including the Industrial Security Sector, which specialize in helping departments with security aspects within contracting.

These groups within PSPC can provide guidance and assistance on how best to proceed, depending on the particularities of any initiative.

PSPC provides advice and guidance to departments, contractors and potential contractors on the security requirements of contracts that require access to sensitive information and assets. This includes contracts for goods, services, construction, building services and leases.

When in doubt, departments should consult the Security requirements for contracting with the Government of Canada on all matters relating to contract security.
