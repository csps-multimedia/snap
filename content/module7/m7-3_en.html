In Conclusion:

  • All contractors and other individuals requiring access to Protected or Classified information or assets must go through a screening process. Additionally, for those contracted individuals who will not have access to Protected or Classified information, but will work within a sensitive area, they must be site access screened.
  • A Security Requirements Checklist Form is used to identify the security requirements associated with a contract.

Here are some examples of questions in the checklist:

  • Will the supplier and its employees require access to Protected and/or Classified information or assets?
  • Will the supplier and its employees require access to restricted-access areas?
  • May unscreened personnel be used for portions of the work?
