A construction company is being considered for a remodelling contract at a Government of Canada building in New Brunswick. The procurement officer is concerned that the employees of the company will need access to an area where protected information and assets are stored.

What screening process should the procurement officer request for the bidding construction company?

Your answer is correct!

The procurement officer should request a Designated Organization Screening (DOS).

A Designated Organization Screening is an administrative determination that an organization is eligible, from a security viewpoint, to access Protected information and assets.
