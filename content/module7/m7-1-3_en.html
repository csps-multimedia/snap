Security Requirements Checklist

The Security Requirements Checklist Form is used to identify the level of security required by the contract to ensure that the appropriate security screening of individuals has taken place and that appropriate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of sensitive information and assets that may be entrusted to the contractor.

Different departments may have additional criteria to meet with respect to security requirements to be integrated into departmental contracts. Your departmental security services can review the checklist prior to any formal contracting being initiated and provide additional guidance.

Departments must use the checklist to define the security requirements for contracts for which PSPC is the contracting authority. Departments should also use the checklist or similar processes for contracts for which they are the contracting authority. If the checklist is not being used, departments should provide written assurance if there are no security requirements, by certifying such on the actual requisition/contract.
