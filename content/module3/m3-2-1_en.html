Updates

The current update cycles are 10 years for reliability status and Secret clearance and 5 years for Top Secret clearance. Individuals will be required to complete the security screening form to account for changes since the last time a security screening was done and to reconfirm their consent agreeing to the necessary verifications of their personal information.

At a minimum, updates involve:

  • Updated application form covering period since last update
  • Financial inquiry
  • Law enforcement inquiry
  • A CSIS security assessment (for security clearances)
