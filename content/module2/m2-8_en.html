Risks and Risk Management

There is no such thing as perfect security: security is all about managing risks.

Few employees are actually involved in assessing security risks and selecting and implementing appropriate security measures.

Security risk management is the responsibility of program managers and security professionals. The aim is to ensure that security supports priorities, plans and programs within each department.
