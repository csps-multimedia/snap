Module Introduction

Managers at all levels, along with accredited human resources advisors (HR), have a role to play with regard to organization and classification (OC).

This module presents the basic principles of organizational design to assist you in carrying out your duties and in fulfilling your responsibilities in this field. The next modules set out the principles associated with job descriptions and classification decisions.

This training is mandatory for those who will have responsibility or authority for organization and classification, as outlined in the Organization and Classification Learning Curriculum.
