Insider Threat

Among deliberate threats one of the most important are insider threats.

It is important to pay special attention to insider threats because:

1

They may come from current or former employees, contractors, or business partners.

2

They may have direct access to sensitive information or authority to perform privileged or powerful functions.

3

They could intentionally misuse access or authority in a manner that either breaches the privacy of personal information or negatively affects the confidentiality, integrity, privacy or availability of the organization's information.

4

Insiders can use their knowledge to facilitate attacks or conspire with external attackers.

5

They are very difficult to detect.
