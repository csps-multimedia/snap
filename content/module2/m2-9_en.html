New threats emerge daily, so it is important that, as government employees, you remain vigilant in your efforts to stay informed and to follow proper security procedures in your workplace.

Let’s review some key points:

  • Security is everyone’s business: we all have a role to play and we all need to be aware of our responsibilities and of the types of threats that are relevant in our environments
  • Find out what security training and awareness programs exist in your department
  • Insider threats are hard to detect; watch for the signs
  • Be careful when handling government information and assets. Accidents can happen and can lead to disclosures. If it happens to you, your departmental security office is there to support you
  • Report any suspicious activities or behaviours to your manager or departmental security office so appropriate action may be taken
