Welcome to Module 3 – Understanding Threats and Risks

At a government-wide level, security threats, risks and incidents must be proactively managed to help protect the government's critical assets, information and services, as well as national security. (Policy on Government Security)

This module will provide you with an overview of the common security threats that could affect the Government of Canada.

Objectives

Upon completion of this session, you will be able to:

  • Recognize common security threats
  • Identify the appropriate actions to safeguard against these threats
