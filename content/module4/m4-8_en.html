Control of Access

Access controls ensure that only authorized personnel, who have been screened at the appropriate level and have a need to know, have access to assets within a facility or restricted areas. This is done by screening visitors and material at entry points by personnel, guards or automated means and, where required, escorting them within the facility or restricted areas.

Authorized access to restricted-access areas should be limited to individuals with the appropriate security screening and the “need to know” or need to access.

Need to know

The need for someone to access and know information in order to perform his or her duties. “Need to Know” and the appropriate screening level correspond with the level of information and/or asset being accessed.
