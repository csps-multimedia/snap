Abbreviations

Purpose

Abbreviations are word reductions and the full definition of the word must be available to someone who does not know its meaning.

All abbreviations are indexed centrally in a file and are dynamically added to the pages where they are referenced.

You can access the complete list of abbreviations for a course from the toolbox on the top bar and select "Resources".

Example

Gross domestic product (GDP) is a monetary measure of the market value of all final goods and services produced in a period (quarterly or yearly) of time.
