External Links

Purpose

External links are quite simply links that lead to websites outside the course.

Clicking on those links will open the website in a new window. There are different settings linked to how the window will open to be discussed with a developper.

All links should be indexed properly in the Resources page to work.

To access the list of index links, simply open the resources inside the toolbox.

Example

When you want to find something, Google is the place to go; it is thus an external website.
