Use Caution in Public Spaces

You should not handle or discuss sensitive information in public spaces.

  • Be aware of your environment when talking about sensitive information in public.
  • Use a more secure space and ensure the people near the area cannot eavesdrop on sensitive conversations.
  • You should never view protected or classified information in public areas such as on the bus or on an airplane. Beware of “shoulder surfing”.

Shoulder Surfing

Looking over someone’s shoulder to get/view the information without their knowledge.
