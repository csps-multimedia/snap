Cabinets

The type of container required to store and secure sensitive information varies based on the level of sensitivity of the information and the physical location of the cabinet.

Many commercial cabinets and locks can be easily tampered with and may or may not be suitable for information that is Protected B or above. You need to ensure you use the proper approved container. You should seek further guidance from your manager or departmental security office if you are unsure.

Cabinets storing sensitive information should be locked at all times when not under the watch of the person who is authorized to open the cabinet.
