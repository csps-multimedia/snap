Security Incidents Involving the Compromise of Information

The loss or compromise (or suspected) of Protected or Classified information constitutes a security incident that must be reported.

If you see evidence of a potential security incident (such as tampering with a secure container, suspicious e-mail, or suspicious behaviour) or suspect a security incident has happened, REPORT IT immediately to your local security officials or departmental security office.

Key Message

It is extremely important to report security incidents immediately because a security incident could cause a significant injury to government operations, individuals or other organizations. Security incidents involving sensitive personal information could cause harm or embarrassment detrimental to the individual's career, reputation, financial position, safety, health or well-being. The sooner the incident is discovered and reported, the more likely it is that the injury can be prevented or minimized.
