Cabinet Confidences

The term “Cabinet confidences” refers to Confidences of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada.

Access to Cabinet confidences must be restricted to authorized individuals who have a valid security screening at the appropriate level and have a need to know.

If you deal with Cabinet confidences, speak with your departmental security office regarding proper procedures. More information is available at Confidences of the Queen's Privy Council for Canada (Cabinet confidences).
