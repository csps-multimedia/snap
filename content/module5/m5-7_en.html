Disposal and Destruction of Sensitive Information

Hardcopy

All sensitive hardcopy information should be disposed of using approved shredders.

Electronic Media

Computers and electronic media including disk drives, laptops, fax machines, BlackBerry’s, photocopiers as well as portable media such as USB keys, hard drives and CDs must be “sanitized and/or destroyed” using approved methods before they are disposed of or reused in a different security environment. It is not enough to simply delete or erase the files because the data can still be recovered.

Special processes are in place within each department for the sanitization of portable storage devices. Contact your IT security personnel for guidance on properly disposing of electronic Media.

Note:

Information should be deleted in accordance with information management recordkeeping and archival policies. You cannot delete or dispose of any information resources that are required for an Access to Information or Privacy request, litigation or official investigation.
