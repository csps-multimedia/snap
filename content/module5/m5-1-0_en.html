Information Management

As employees of the government, we have a responsibility to follow information management best practices and to ensure information management security throughout its life cycle. We need to make certain that the appropriate records relating to our work, whatever the form, are categorized and protected in the appropriate manner.

As you go about your normal business activities each day, you generate and collect paper and electronic information resources. These information resources provide an important record of the actions you have taken, the decisions you have made, and the reasons for both, allowing for transparency and accountability.
