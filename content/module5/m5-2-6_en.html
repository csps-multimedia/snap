Other Types of Assets and Services

Although this section focuses on information, it is important to note that other types of assets also require protection. Assets may be tangible (such as computer systems, portable media storage devices, filing cabinets, real property, or cash) or intangible (such as trust, public confidence and international reputation).

Tangible assets often need to be protected according to the sensitivity of the information they contain. Some assets require protection based on the intrinsic value of the asset itself (e.g. cash, heritage items, badges, etc.).

Security also plays an important role in the continued delivery of critical services.
