Locks

Separate locks may be required for storing sensitive information, depending on the cabinet. Be sure to secure the lock before leaving your office.

Should a key be lost or any part of the hardware compromised, contact your security personnel immediately.

It is normally the role of security personnel to set up combination locks to avoid any easily guessed numbers, and ensure the lock can be opened in the event the user forgets the combination or after the user departs the organization. The combination/code must only to be given to authorized users. Please verify the process within your organization. Combinations and codes should be changed at a minimum once a year and or when there is a change of ownership. Combination and codes can be stored as a back-up in a different secure cabinet that is approved to protect at least the same level of information.

Key Message

You need to ensure you use the proper locks. Do not hesitate to seek further guidance if you are unsure.
