Types of Records

Official records are retained and must be assigned a security category and protected accordingly. Even transitory records can be sensitive and may need to be disposed of in a secure manner.

For more information regarding information management best practices, refer to the Guidelines for Employees of the Government of Canada: Information Management (IM) Basics.

Contact your departmental information management specialists to learn more about the practices and procedures that apply in your organization.

Key Message

All information resources must be reviewed to determine if their content is sensitive; if so, it must be protected in a manner that is equivalent with its level of sensitivity.
