Welcome

It is important that all public servants have a foundational understanding of how Canada's non-partisan federal public service serves the democratically elected government of the day.

Who We Work For examines the functions and the organizational relationships among Parliament, the Government of Canada, and the federal public service. Participants will explore the role of government, how Canada governs itself, the organization of the federal government, the functions of departments and central agencies, and common government services.

Skip to the test

Learners have the option to attempt the requisite end-of-course test without having to take the course. This option is for those who think that they have the necessary knowledge to successfully pass the final test without first going through the course material. The pass mark is 70%. Nonetheless, it is strongly recommended that you go through the entire course.

You may skip to the test here, or you may select the next button to proceed to the next module.
