Course Objectives and Outline

Objectives

Upon completion of this course, you will have the basic knowledge to:

  • recognize the 8 security controls that are outlined in the Policy on Government Security and supporting policy instruments and how they apply in your day‑to‑day activities with a view to mitigating security risks
  • summarize your responsibilities in support of the protection of information, individuals and assets, as outlined in the Policy on Government Security
  • identify and report any potential security concerns

Course Outline

The course is divided into 10 modules, including a final assessment, and should take approximately 2.5 hours to complete. Use the “Next” button to navigate the course sequentially. If you wish, you may use the top navigational menu bar to navigate among the modules or to return to previously-read material at any time. The final module is an assessment of the knowledge learned. Upon achieving a score of 70% or higher, you will gain access to a certificate you can print up to display your successful completion of this course.
