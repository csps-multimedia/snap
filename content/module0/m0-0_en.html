Introduction

Welcome to Introduction to Security Awareness, Module 1 of the online security awareness course.

The goal of this course is to provide you with the basic knowledge needed to understand the importance of security in the federal government. It is also intended as a reminder of your responsibilities toward the protection of information, assets and the workplace, as outlined in the various Government of Canada policies and guidelines.

The course is designed for all individuals working in departments of the Government of Canada, as defined in section 2; and in entities included in Schedules IV and V of the Financial Administration Act (FAA), unless excluded by specific acts, regulations or orders in council. It was created by the Canada School of Public Service (CSPS) in collaboration with Correctional Service Canada (CSC), Privy Council Office (PCO), Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat (TBS), as well as many other individuals from other departments and agencies.
