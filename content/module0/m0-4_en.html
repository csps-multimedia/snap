Course Navigation and Instructions

Back and Next

Instead of a navigation bar with the “Back” and “Forward” buttons of a standard browser like Internet Explorer, some of MyAccount courses have "Back” and “Next” navigation buttons (shown as < and >) located at the top and bottom of the right hand side of the screen. Clicking on the “<” (or Back) button will take you to the previously viewed page and clicking on the “>” (or Next) button will bring you to the next page.

French Toggle

Click on FR to switch from the English version of the course to the French version.

Course Home

Click on HOME to return to the course Home page.

Help

Click on ? to access these help topics again.

Toolbox

Click on the Toolbox icon to select either Glossary or Resources

  • Glossary:
    • Select Glossary to review definitions of terms related to security.
  • Resources:
    • Click the “Resources” button to access links to tools, guidelines, and other information related to security.

Exit

Click on ‘X’ when you want to exit this course. A bookmark is automatically created to mark your current location in the course. The next time you launch the course you will have return to where you left off.

Navigation Bar – Menu

  • Click the navigation bar to navigate among the course.
  • Click on a module title to see its related sections.
  • Checkmarks will appear next to modules and sections that you have completed.

Video Player:

  • The embedded video player allows you to play, fast forward, rewind, adjust sound settings.
  • To view the full transcript, select the Transcript button located below the video player.
